2. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to all users of the IronTier mobile application, whether using the app as a guest (without an account) or as a signed-in user with a linked Google account.

3. Information We Collect

3a. Guest Mode (No Account Required)

When you use IronTier without creating an account, all data is stored exclusively on your device using local on-device storage (SQLite). We do not transmit, collect, or have access to any of this data. This includes:

  • Workout sessions (exercise names, sets, reps, weights, dates, notes)
  • Personal records (best estimated 1-rep max, heaviest set, best set volume per exercise)
  • Routines and routine templates
  • Profile settings (display name, bodyweight, height, age, sex, preferred units)
  • Strength ranks and achievements (trophies)

Guest-mode data remains on your device until you delete the app, perform a factory reset, or create an account and choose to migrate your data.

3b. Signed-In Mode (Google Sign-In)

When you create an account via Google Sign-In, the following happens:

From Google Sign-In, we receive:

  • Your Google account identifier (a numeric user ID)
  • Your name (as set in your Google account)
  • Your email address (used solely for account identification; we do not use it for marketing)
  • We do not receive your Google password

You voluntarily provide and we store in the cloud (Supabase):

  • Display name and username (chosen by you, visible to others if you add friends)
  • Bodyweight (in kg or lbs, converted to kg for storage)
  • Height (in cm)
  • Age
  • Sex (used only for strength standard calculations — this is health-related data; see Section 5)
  • Preferred units (kg/lb)
  • Workout history: workout names, notes, start/end times, total volume
  • Exercise data: sets, reps, weights, set type (warmup/working/drop/failure)
  • Personal records (best e1RM, heaviest set, best set volume per exercise, dates achieved)
  • Body measurements (if you choose to log them)
  • Routines and routine templates
  • Achievement/trophy history

Automatically collected:

  • Device platform (iOS or Android), used only for authentication flow compatibility
  • App usage events (e.g., workout started, workout completed) are not currently collected or transmitted to any analytics service

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core app functionality: tracking workouts, calculating personal records, displaying strength progression charts
  • Calculate your strength rank relative to bodyweight-adjusted standards (Brzycki formula and percentage-of-bodyweight thresholds)
  • Sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • Enable social features (friends, groups, leaderboards) when you choose to use them
  • Allow account recovery if you reinstall the app on a new device
  • Authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In

We do not use your data for:

  • Targeted advertising
  • Sale or licensing to third parties
  • Building behavioral profiles for external use
  • Any purpose unrelated to the core fitness-tracking functionality of the app

5. Social Features: Data Visible to Friends and Groups

IronTier includes optional social features (Friends and Groups). These features are entirely opt-in — you must send or accept a friend request, or join a group, for any data to be shared with other users.

When you are connected to another user as a friend, or share a group with them, the following summary data becomes visible to them on leaderboards:

Data fieldVisible to friends/group members
Display name✅ Yes

Bodyweight (kg)✅ Yes — used to calculate strength ratios

Best estimated 1-rep max (Squat, Bench, Deadlift, OHP, Row)✅ Yes

What is never visible to other users:

  • Per-set detail (individual set weights, reps, notes from any specific workout)
  • Your Google email address
  • Body measurements (height, specific measurement logs)
  • Age
  • Workout notes
  • Any data from users who are not your friends or in your groups

If you remove a friend or leave a group, they will no longer have access to your data through those social features.

Note on sensitive data: Bodyweight and sex are health-related data points. They are shared with friends/group members as described above solely to enable comparative strength ranking. If you prefer not to share this information, you can leave the bodyweight and sex fields blank in your profile settings — this will result in rank calculations using default reference values rather than your actual data.

7. Data Retention

Guest mode: Data is stored on your device indefinitely until you delete the app or clear app data.

Signed-in accounts: Your data is stored on Supabase servers for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (see Section 8), your data is permanently deleted from our servers.

We do not retain data backups of deleted accounts.

8. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your IronTier account and all associated data at any time:

  1. Open the App
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

This action is irreversible. Deletion removes:

  • Your profile data from our cloud servers
  • All workout history, sets, personal records, and routines stored in the cloud
  • All social connections (friendships, group memberships)
  • Your authentication credentials

Local data on your device (guest or signed-in) is cleared from the app's local storage. Note that standard device backups (iCloud, Google One) may retain copies of local data separately from our servers — this is outside our control.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Supabase enforces Row-Level Security (RLS) policies so each user can only access their own data, unless explicitly shared via the social features described in Section 5

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
